### Accessing Library

<Steps>
<Step title="Accessing Library">
1. Click on the [Library](https://trickest.io/dashboard/library) tab in the left sidebar.
<Frame>
    <img src="/images/tutorials/tutorials-library.png" alt="Library Workflows" />
</Frame>
</Step>
<Step title='Selecting Workflows '>
2. Click on the `Workflows` tab in the left navigation bar.
</Step>
<Step title='Copying the workflow'>
Find a workflow you want to copy. For example, let's copy `Levels-deep Subdomain Enumeration` workflow.

<Frame>
    <img src="/images/tutorials/copy-workflows-library.gif" alt="Library Workflows" />
</Frame>
</Step>
<Step title='Workflow is copied'>
The workflow is copied to your workspace. You can now edit and run the workflow.
</Step>

</Steps>
